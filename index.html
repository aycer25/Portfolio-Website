About the Author

I'm a graduate from San Jose State University majoring in Professional Technical Writing. My original major was Computer Engineering, but decided to make the switch because I found creating documentation to be much more enjoyable -- not only I get to learn about subject matter, but also help cater towards a specific audience. After all, technical writing is somewhat of a (specialized) customer service job -- you are helping the customer (sometimes companies) how to understand your organization's product!



Hobbies: Exercising, gaming, collecting/building LEGOs, learning new things.
